Article

article a self-contained composition in a doc, page, app, or site which is inteded to be independently distributable or reusable. This could be a forum post a magazine article, blog entry, an object, or any other independent item of content. Each article should be identified by including a heading as a child of the article element.

Aside

aside represents a section of the page with content connected tangentially to the rest, which could be considered separate from that conent. These sections are often represented as sidebars or inserts. They often contail the definitions on the sidebars, such as definitions from the glossary; there may also be other types of info such as related ads, bio of the author, web apps, profile info, or related links on the blog.

Details

details is used as a disclosure widget from which the user can retrieve additional info about the content above.

Figure and Figcaption

figure represents sef-contained content with a caption figcaption.

Example:

figure
img src="cat.jpg"
figcaption cat photo by Moxie. /figcaption
/figure

Footer

footer represents a footer for it's section. It typically contains info about the autor of the section, copyright or links related to the documents.

Header

header represents a group of introductory or navigational aids. May contain heading elements, logo, wrapped section's header, a search for, and so on.

Main

main the main content

Mark

mark represents highlighted text i.e. a run of text marked for reference purpose, due to its relevance in a particular context. For example it can be used in a page showing search results to highlight every instance of the searched-for word.

Nav

nav represents a section of a page that links to other parts within the page. It's a section with navigation links.

Section

section is a generic piece of a document. Everything inside typically has the same theme. Each section should be identified by a heading.
